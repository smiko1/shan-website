About Me

Hey y'all! I'm Shannon and I'm currently 21 years old. I started my fitness journey about 5 years ago when I became obsessed with cardio. Yup, I was one of those "cardio bunnies". Trust me, cardio is amazing and it has SO many benefits. But regardless, I still didn't have the body I wanted. I was lean and I didn't need to lose any weight - in fact, I almost looked too skinny. I was 5'9" and 122 lbs (my all-time low) when I decided to get into lifting. That was about 2 years ago. So here I am, 5'9" and all 150 lbs of me!
As of February 2019, I finished my first successful bulk. I'm now in the process of my first official 'cut', and I plan to put all of my personal experiences with it on the bulking and cutting pages that you can find on the navigation tab above. So if you're thinking about going through a bulking or cutting phase for the first time, go to those pages I just mentioned because I wrote them specifically for you!!
