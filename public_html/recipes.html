Ingredients:
  • 1 lb. lean ground beef (or ground turkey if you roll that way)
  • 1 1/2 tsp. minced garlic or garlic powder
  • 1/4 c. packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 c. low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tsp. sesame oil
  • 1/4 tsp. ground ginger
  • 1/4 tsp. red pepper flakes (or more if you like SPICY)
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 1-2 bags cauliflower rice (or 2 c. actual rice as a substitute)

Directions:
  1. In a pot or skillet, cook the ground beef and garlic. Drain excess fat once cooked through.
  2. Meanwhile, in a cup, mix together brown sugar, soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, red pepper flakes, and pepper.
  3. Pour mixture over the cooked ground beef and let simmer for another few minutes.
  4. Cook your rice to your liking and top it with the ground beef.

Some Side Notes: (1) if you're not afraid of a few extra calories, use actual rice! (2) top with plain greek yogurt and be AMAZED (3) if you're like me, uh.. you're gonna wanna double a recipe. Kay that's it :)

Ingredients: Waffles:
  • 1 1/2 c. buttermilk
  • 4 tbsp. melted butter
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 1/2 c. all-purpose flour
  • 1 c. yellow cornmeal
  • 2 tbsp. sugar
  • 2 tsp. baking soda
  • 2 tsp. taco seasoning
  • 1 tsp. salt (or Old Bay seasoning if you're from the Maryland area and know what that is!)
Topping:
  • 1 lb. ground beef (or ground turkey)
  • 2/3 c. water
  • remaining taco seasoning
  • 1 4 oz. can diced green chiles
  • (OPTIONAL) chopped lettuce, salsa, plain greek yogurt, guac, shredded cheese, tomatoes, onion, and/or cilantro

Directions:
  1. Preheat your waffle iron.
  2. In a mixing bowl, stir together buttermilk, butter and eggs.
  3. Stir in flour, cornmeal, sugar, baking soda, taco seasoning, and salt.
  4. Spray the waffle iron with cooking spray and cook them waffles!!
  5. While the waffles are cooking, heat a skillet on medium-high heat and cook the ground beef until fully cooked. Drain excess fat.
  6. Stir in the water and taco seasoning, and heat until boiling.
  7. Reduce the heat and cook until thickened (just a few minutes). Add in green chiles.
  8. Once the waffles are done, top them with the ground beef mixture and any other toppings you want!

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 packet taco seasoning
  • 2-4 chicken breasts, cooked and shredded (pressure cooking it beforehand is the best!)
  • 2 cans petite diced tomatoes
  • 2 can black beans
  • 2 cans corn
  • 2 bags cauliflower rice
  • 1/2 onion (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder (optional)

Directions:
  1. Pressure cook (or saute if you don't own a pressure cooker) the chicken. Season it for more flavor!
  2. Shred the chicken (or dice it) once cooked all the way.
  3. (OPTIONAL) Defrost the cauliflower rice in the microwave so it's not a huge frozen chunk when you're trying to stir it into everything else.
  4. In a big pot on medium-high heat, add everything. Order doesn't really matter.
  5. Cook until everything is hot and that's it!
  6. You can either put this into your meal prep containers and refirgerate/freeze for later use, or you can eat it right away. Enjoy!

Side Note: Tastes EXTRA amazing topped with salsa, plain greek yogurt, and guac!!!

Directions: Put it all in your nutribullet or whatever blender thing you have and blend away! Make sure you don't forget the pudding mix. It makes the shake extra THICCCCC. At least 5 C's, if not more.

Ingredients:
  • 1 box Nilla Wafers
  • 4 ripe bananas
  • 2 4 oz. boxes vanilla pudding mix (Jello brand tastes best)
  • 2 c. skim milk
  • 1 8 oz. tub whipped topping
  • 1 c. plain greek yogurt (I use this instead of sour cream)
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract

Directions:
  1. Crush 1/2-2/3 of the box of Nilla Wafers and spread them along the bottom of a 9x13 dish.
  2. Line the dish with the rest of the Nilla Wafers (make sure you don't crush too many of them so that there are enough!).
  3. Slice the 4 bananas and layer them on top of the crumbs on the bottom of the pan.
  4. In a mixing bowl, stir together the pudding and milk, and then add the whipped topping, yogurt, and vanilla extract.
  5. Pour the mixture over the sliced bananas and spread evenly.
  6. If you have remaining Nilla Wafers, crush some up and sprinkle it on top.
  7. Refrigerate until you're ready to eat it!
